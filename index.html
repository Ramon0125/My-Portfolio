    Who am i?

    I'm Ramón E. Lebrón, a Web application developer and Database Administrator

    I am a freelance professional based in the Dominican Republic, specializing in software development with extensive experience in advanced technological solutions. I am proficient in tools like Visual Studio, Visual Studio Code, Dynamics, and Power BI, approaching projects with a comprehensive and efficient mindset.

    I have managed complex databases with MySQL and SQL Server, ensuring their performance and security. Additionally, I am an expert in creating high-performance web applications using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, PHP, and C#/.NET, delivering scalable and customized solutions.

    I possess deep knowledge of REST APIs, enabling efficient integration of web services, and solid experience in testing, ensuring the delivery of high-quality, error-free solutions. My focus is on understanding the specific needs and goals of each company, ensuring that IT systems operate with maximum efficiency and align perfectly with corporate objectives.
